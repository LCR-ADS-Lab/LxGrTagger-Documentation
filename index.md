# LxGrTagger Annotation Guidelines

- This document provides the annotation guidelines for the tags used in [LxGrTagger](https://github.com/kristopherkyle/LxGrTgr). Each `tag` is explained with descriptions and examples.

- The guidelines are primarily based on *Longman Grammar of Spoken and Written English* by Biber et al. (1999). Content is paraphrased with referenced page numbers, and additional sources are cited with corresponding pages.
